    • Biography

    Welcome to my Bio page where you get to learn a little more about me! I am a Professional Writing (PW) major and I came from a town whose high school was called Pewamo-Westphalia (PW). So when my college advisor asked me why I wanted to be a PW major I obviously felt it was meant to be (but not really), let's rewind a bit. Ever since I was little I have loved to read. I begged my mom to teach me to read and I loved reading to my classmates and aunts and uncles. You can imagine my mother was a bit enthused and vexed when I made a long distance phone call to Texas (back when landlines were the most popular form of communication) just to read a story to my aunt. Sometime in early elementary school I started to write my own stories. In sixth grade I had my own short story published, which was exciting. I have a deep desire to write books that take one on an adventure.
